Knowing Yourself

2026-03-26

I believe in strong dopamine hits - for example, I am creating blogs as a way to help others and myself. To make it more convenient for myself to blog, I integrated Apple actions to GitHub dispatch, so that my notes can become part of my website. By the way, while typing the above, I just realized that I can use the GitHub mobile app, or perhaps there already exists better ways of doing this, but I wanted to do it my way and I did it.

There is another idea I just got interested in - the art of staying mentally active. I don't know about others, but I often perform based on my mental state, which I think is something I'll look into changing. A habit to be consistently in a strong, and positive mood is essential.

I had another realization: doing things my way is exactly what I am best at.

A key lesson to learn is that I believe jotting down your thoughts is the way to understand yourself. You can try journaling or other methods to see what works best for you.

Some key takeaways to live by include: * Be humble * Be grateful * Enjoy life

Cheers!
