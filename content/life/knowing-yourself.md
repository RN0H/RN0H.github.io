I believe in strong dopamine hits - for example, I am creating blogs as a way to help others and myself. To make it more convenient for myself to blog, I integrated Apple Actions with GitHub Dispatch, so that my notes can become part of my website. By the way, while typing the above, I just realized that I can use the GitHub mobile app, or perhaps there already exists better ways of doing this, but I wanted to do it my way and I did it.

This experience led to another realization: I am best at doing things my way. 

The key lesson to learn from this is that **jotting down your thoughts** is a great way to understand yourself. By reflecting on my own experiences and projects, I have gained valuable insights into my strengths and motivations.

Some key takeaways from this journey include:
* Embracing personal projects as a way to learn and grow
* Using tools like [GitHub](https://github.com) to streamline workflows
* The importance of **self-reflection** and writing down your thoughts

Cheers! Be humble, grateful, and enjoy life!
